Felipe Jun Iglesia

About Me

Malaysian pianist, arranger, and composer for film and video games Felipe Jun Iglesia revels in his passion for music. He has been in many roles, including being the President, Producer, Arranger, and Music Director for the International College of Music's Video Game Tribute (VGT) Concert in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. The concert brought in around 400 audience members with music that was mainly prepared and conducted by Felipe himself. He now studies in Berklee College of Music under Film and Games Scoring to hone his craft passion in scoring for the visual arts. He has scored for a number of games, ranging from personal collaborative projects to ongoing development teams in UCLA and is now setting food in to the Hollywood industry. Felipe continues to improve in his ability as a truly well-rounded musicians and find opportunities to lead and collaborate. His ongoing projects include scoring for visuals and continuing his legacy with VGT in the United States.
